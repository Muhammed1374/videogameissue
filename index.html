Are Video Games That Bad?

Video games have been a subject of controversy for decades,
with some people claiming they are harmful while others argue they have positive effects.
But today we will discuss this intense topic in a detailed simple manner.

Check out our PowerPoint presentation for more information on the topic of video games and their effects!

🟢Positive Effects 🔴Negative Effects
✅Improves decision-making speed ⚠️Can cause insomnia if played late at night
✅Enhances problem-solving skills ⚠️Can become addictive if overplayed
✅Develops strategic thinking ⚠️May reduce physical activity
✅Improves hand-eye coordination ⚠️May cause eye strain
✅Encourages teamwork and communication (multiplayer games) ⚠️Excessive gaming can impact academic or work performance
✅Increases creativity and imagination ⚠️Some games may expose players to toxic online behavior
✅Improves multitasking and reaction time ⚠️Spending too much time gaming can reduce face-to-face social interaction

Video games can have both positive and negative effects on individuals,
depending on various factors such as the type of game, the amount of time spent playing,
and the individual's personality and circumstances.
